OUR DOMAIN:

Optimizing Emergency Response in a City

A city is represented as a graph, where:

• Nodes represent key locations such as hospitals, fire stations, police stations, residential areas, and commercial buildings.

• Edges represent roads connecting these locations, each with a weight representing the travel time (in minutes).

Scenario:

The city government wants to optimize its emergency response system by analyzing the layout and ensuring quick access to essential services during emergencies.
